QUALITY AND COMMITMENT

  • In strict adherence to HACCP quality guidelines of USFDA & EU standards, all the processes (procurement,
    production, processing) are carried out in complete professional supervision.
  • Our inbuilt laboratories are upgraded from time to time to perform tests with the product in all levels.
  • RIE owned purchase centers, vehicles, Ice plants & manpower enable us to go for direct procurement. This helps in
    lessening the handling of the material, which in turn results in a better quality product.
  • RIE ensure that every product is made in a hygienic environment and every delivery is made with utmost care.
  • Strategy of raw material procurement has been a strength for RIE.
  • We procure the raw materials directly from the farmers with no middlemen.
